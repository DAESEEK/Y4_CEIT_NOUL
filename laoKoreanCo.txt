	<div class="entry-content single-entry-content">
		<h1 class="p1"><span class="s1"><strong>Department of Korean Language</strong></span></h1>
<p><img class="alignnone wp-image-2031 size-full" src="http://35.197.147.51/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/1920137_croped.jpeg" alt="" width="4295" height="2612" srcset="http://lkclaos.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/1920137_croped.jpeg 4295w, http://lkclaos.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/1920137_croped-300x182.jpeg 300w, http://lkclaos.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/1920137_croped-768x467.jpeg 768w, http://lkclaos.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/1920137_croped-1024x623.jpeg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 4295px) 100vw, 4295px"/></p>
<h5></h5>
<h5 class="p1"><span class="s1"><strong>Department Overview</strong></span></h5>
<p class="p1"><span class="s3">Currently, Korean courses are necessary to do business with Koreans in Laos, to study abroad, to gain work opportunities in Korea, and to marry Koreans. Therefore, we have opened this department to solve this problem. </span><span class="s4">The Lao people can live with the Koreans without inconvenience because they understand the Korean culture from the perspective of science, understand the Korean culture, and teach various ways to adapt in Korea.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>This will be what the Korean professors will do in the Korean language department.</span></p>
<h5></h5>
<h5 class="p2"><span class="s4"><strong>Educational Objectives</strong></span></h5>
<ul>
<li class="p2" style="text-align: left;"><span class="s4">To cultivate Korean personnel in Laos to help resolve communication issues with Lao people</span></li>
<li class="p2"><span class="s4">To promote a multicultural society and cultivate human resources that will help Korean families living in Laos</span></li>
<li class="p2"><span class="s4">To foster global professionals to help Lao people living in Korea or within the Korean culture</span></li>
<li class="p2"><span class="s4">To foster global professionals with the flexibility to work in various Korean and overseas jobs</span></li>
</ul>
<h5></h5>
<h5><strong>Main Subjects</strong></h5>
<ul>
<li>Korean pronunciation theory</li>
<li>Korean grammar theory</li>
<li>Introduction to the Korean language</li>
<li>Modern Korean culture</li>
<li>Traditional Korean culture</li>
<li>Korean business language, etc.</li>
</ul>
<h5></h5>
<h5><span class="s5"><b>Employment status and graduate career status</b></span></h5>
<p class="p4"><span class="s4">Graduates from the Department of Korean language will be engaged in interpretation, translation, trade, and educational services within institutions such as banks, government offices, and general enterprises.</span></p>
